About SSP Enterprises

Established in 2016 in Sagar,Karnataka we,SSP Enterprises are noteworthy manufacturers, suppliers and wholesale distributors and exporters of banana paper leaf, Silver coated paper plates and Disposable Silver Coated Paper Dona / Bowl and all types of disposable plates. The best quality papers are used for the purpose of manufacturing of the offered paper products.

Known for its rich attributes such as energy efficiency, durability, high functionality, noise-free operation, minimum maintenance and high productivity, the offered range of paper products are designed as per the set industry norms and regulations. Ability to meet the bulk orders, transparent transaction policy and prompt delivery of the offered range has helped us in attaining a commendable position in this domain. In addition to this, keeping the convenience of our customers in mind, we accept different modes of payment.
